Fanart of Camp Dragonhead from Final Fantasy XIV, created in a 3D pixel art style inspired by Octopath Traveler. Scene created in Unreal Engine 5. All 3D assets modeled in Blender. Textures and character sprites created in Pyxel Edit and Photoshop. Finalist in the 2023 FFXIV Fanfest Art Contest
