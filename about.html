About Me

Hello, and thanks for taking a look at my portfolio. I'm a game developer with extensive experience working on various small indie game projects. Currently, I am looking for new opportunities to join exciting game projects, while also working on my own projects and polishing my skills.

I got my start in game dev as a level design student at The Guildhall at SMU. Before Guildhall, I worked as a technical writer. Outside of game development, I enjoy reading, tabletop games, and fencing. I have about 20 years' worth of fencing experience, and have helped instruct workshops on stage fighting and historical swordplay.

My professional background and interests have given me a unique viewpoint on game design, which has allowed me to be a helpful contributor on small development teams.
