Derailed - Half-Life 2 Level

A Half-Life 2 mod I made in 2023 to practice my design and world-building skills. My goal was to make a short, well-paced singleplayer level. In "Derailed," the player must escape a zombie-infested railway tunnel, emerging into the light only to find themselves in a three-way battle between Combine soldiers and a horde of zombies.

Whitebox and early layout thumbnail sketches.
